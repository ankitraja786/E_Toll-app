                                           
                                             E_Toll- app

Worked on this  project in a hackathon  with a group of peers.

created an Android app through whch a person can pay the toll tax of his journey before  reaching the toll booth .with image  processing ,
the vehicle number will be read and then  checked in our database .on positive response ,the vehicle will be allowed to pass.

used android stdio ,java, google maps SDK,Razorpay Android SDk


Razorpay  - It gives you access to all payment modes including credit card, debit card, netbanking, UPI and popular wallets including JioMoney, Mobikwik, Airtel Money, FreeCharge, Ola Money and PayZapp.
